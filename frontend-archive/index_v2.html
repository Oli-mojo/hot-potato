The 60-second version

One potato. One throne. One growing Pot. You snipe the throne from whoever's currently sitting on it by paying their asking price. You sit. You set a new (higher) asking price. Someone eventually snipes you — and when they do, you walk away with the ETH they paid plus a slice of the Pot, depending on how long you sat.

The longer you sit, the bigger your Pot slice. Cold = 0%. Warm = 1%. Hot = 3%. Blazing = 7%. Legendary = 15%.

What you actually get when you buy

  • The Hot Potato NFT — a single, one-of-one ERC-721 on Base.
  • The throne itself — a fresh timer starts ticking the moment you take it.
  • A chance to ride the sit-tier ladder up to Legendary for a 15% Pot slice when you're eventually sniped.
  • A souvenir NFT automatically minted to you when you're sniped, tagged with the potato's stage at that moment.

What "sprouting" means

The longer you hold, the more you sprout — and each stage raises your minimum ask floor. Sprouting fortifies the throne: a Bloomed potato costs +50% more to snipe than a Fresh one, an Eternal potato costs +200% more. You're harder to dislodge the longer you sit. On top of that, every sale while you're holding also accrues into the Last Supper Reserve, which pays out to whoever is sitting at game-end — tier-weighted. Sprouting is the anticipation: fortify, survive, collect.

Four things spectators can do

Don't want to risk sniping the throne? You can still play.

🌱 Root on a Holder

Stake ETH on whoever's currently sitting. If they survive another tier upgrade, your stake earns a multiplier. If they get sniped before the next tier, your stake is forfeit (and goes to the Pot).

👁 Glance

Pay 0.001 ETH to reveal the current holder's recent asking-price trajectory — did they just raise, hold, or cut? Fee → Pot.

🎯 Limit Buy

Place a limit order: auto-snipe me in if the min-ask crosses X. A keeper bot executes for a small bounty paid out of your deposit. Useful for picking off fresh holders before their floor lifts.

🗳 Vote to Seal

Spectators can collectively stake glance-votes to force the Last Supper early. Once the stake pool crosses ~10% of the Reserve, LAST_SUPPER begins and a 48h window opens. Lets the crowd drain a squatter.

🗳 Souvenir Vote

Weekly community vote picks one trait for the next souvenir mint. Voting weight = your past activity.

How the game ends

The contract enters LAST_SUPPER state when either trigger fires, whichever comes first:

  • Timer path — 30 days without a transfer. A permanent squatter gets a natural death.
  • Spectator path — glance-vote stakes cross ~10% of the Reserve. The crowd forces the endgame.

Once in Last Supper, a 48-hour window opens for one last round of snipes. At the end of the window, anyone can call finalSeal() and the Reserve pays out.

Tier-weighted seal payout. Whoever sits at seal collects 10% / 25% / 50% / 75% / 100% of the Reserve based on their final sit-tier. The residual rolls forward to Season 2 as its opening Reserve.

The Savior NFT

If a single snipe during the 48h Last Supper window takes the throne from a Cold/Warm sitter and the new buyer climbs a tier before seal, the buyer gets a one-of-one Savior NFT. In-game medal, bragging rights only.

After SEALED

Once finalSeal() fires, the contract freezes in place. Souvenirs remain tradeable. Season 2 spawns with whatever residual rolled over.

Do I get the Pot when I buy?

No. You get the throne and a fresh timer. Your Pot slice is earned when someone later snipes you.

Can the current holder just sit forever?

No — two things stop them. First, the 30-day no-transfer timer forces Last Supper automatically. Second, spectators can collectively stake glance-votes to trigger the endgame early. A squatter wins only if they stay sitting through the 48h Last Supper window, and even then only collects their tier-weighted share of the Reserve.

Is everything on-chain?

Yes. Pot balance, sit-tier payouts, sprouting stages, and Last Supper state all live on the contract. The website is a view layer — if we go offline, the game keeps running.
