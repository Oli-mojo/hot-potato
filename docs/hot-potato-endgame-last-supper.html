🥔 HOT POTATO

The Seller's Take & The Endgame

Payout resolution · Terminal-state design · "The Last Supper" mechanic · April 2026
Superseded · 2026-04-20

Endgame in this doc is "100% of the Pot to the Eternal Holder." That's no longer the design. Live model (A + partial C): a Last Supper Reserve accrues alongside the live Pot; at finalSeal() the current sitter collects a tier-weighted share (Cold 10% → Legendary 100%) and the residual rolls to Season 2. Last Supper is triggered by a 30-day no-transfer timer OR spectator vote-stake crossing ~10% of Reserve. Canonical numbers: hot-potato-v4-mechanic-spec.html.

1. What The Seller Walks Away With

"The seller" = the outgoing holder, the one who just got sniped. Their payout is a stack of three pieces. Everything below is settled on-chain inside buyPotato().

1.3 The Souvenir NFT

One freshly minted Souvenir NFT — rarity rolled on-chain from sit-tier plus any pending boosts (overpay, loyalty, referral, trade-in, Sprout). Minted to the outgoing holder's wallet in the same transaction.

The reframe: in the old design, being sniped was losing the potato. In this design, being sniped is cashing out your position. You bought a seat, you sat on it, and the game paid you.

3. When No One Buys — Three Time Horizons

"What happens when no one buys" has three different answers depending on how long "no one" lasts. The Sprouting system handles the short-to-medium horizon. The Treasury Backstop handles the medium. The Last Supper handles the terminal case.

Short silence DAY 0 – 7

Normal quiet. Holder's sit-timer grows, Pot slice grows, nothing unusual. Healthy resting state.

Stage 3 · Sprouted DAY 7 – 14

Asking price decays 5%/day automatically. Any buyer gets +1 Sprout Boost on their next souvenir. The potato is now publicly on sale at a discount — the crowd comes hunting.

Stage 4 · Bloomed DAY 14 – 21

Decay accelerates to 10%/day. Sprout Boost jumps to +2. Spectator limit-buy orders start firing as the price crosses their thresholds. At this point a sale is very likely.

Stage 5 · Harvest Window DAY 21 – 30

Holder can call harvest() — voluntarily end the hold, mint a guaranteed-Legendary "Grower" souvenir, reset the potato at 25% of last price. Dignified exit.

Treasury Buyback DAY 30

Anyone in the world can call treasuryBuyback(). Treasury purchases the potato at 40% of last price. Holder receives Tier 4 Pot slice + a "Released" souvenir. Potato re-lists at a reset price. Caller gets a 0.005 ETH keeper bounty.

Normal cycle resumes IF TREASURY FIRES

After a treasury buyback, the potato is fresh again. New starting price, new owner (the treasury itself — which holds it until first buyer arrives, usually within minutes).

So far so good — the game self-heals through one lull. But what if the lull never breaks? What if the treasury fires, no one buys again, it fires again, and the game keeps refusing to spark?

4. The Last Supper — Terminal Endgame

This is the mechanic you've been planning for: the actual end of Hot Potato. Triggered when the game has run out of momentum and none of the smaller self-heal mechanisms work anymore.

4.1 Trigger Condition

The Last Supper enters when either:

  • The Treasury has fired 3 consecutive buybacks with no intervening player-initiated sale, or
  • The Treasury balance drops below the buyback floor and no sale occurs for 60 days.

Either condition confirms the game has stopped. The contract emits LastSupperBegan and the site flips into a distinctive "Last Supper" visual mode — the potato starts glowing faintly, a 30-day countdown appears.

4.2 The 30-Day Last Supper Window

During this window:

  • The asking price decays linearly to zero over the full 30 days.
  • Anyone on Earth can still buy the potato at the current decayed price. If they do, the contract exits Last Supper mode and the game resumes normally — but the buyer receives a unique "Savior" 1/1 NFT in addition to their standard buy outcome. Being the person who brought the potato back has permanent on-chain bragging rights.
  • The current holder cannot harvest, cannot transfer, cannot sell outside the contract. They just sit and wait.
  • All Rooting, Glance, and Limit-Buy functions continue to work.

4.3 If The 30 Days Pass With No Buyer

The contract calls finalSeal(). The holder becomes The Eternal Holder. The game is over.

Narrative: nobody wanted the potato. Not even for free. The person holding it when the music finally stopped... kept it forever.

5.1 The Seal

When finalSeal() fires:

  • buyPotato() permanently reverts. The potato can never be sold again by anyone.
  • Souvenir minting stops. No new souvenirs can ever be generated.
  • All existing souvenirs remain fully tradeable on OpenSea forever (they're their own ERC-721 tokens).
  • The Eternal Potato token is transferable only by the Eternal Holder — they can sell it on OpenSea if they want. They probably won't. If they do, it becomes the most expensive potato ever sold.
  • Rooting, Glance, and Limit-Buy functions are disabled; any ETH in those escrows is refunded pull-style.

5.2 Why It Matters

A game with a real ending is different from a game without one. Consider the consequences:

  • Late-game holders have extraordinary asymmetric upside. Late in the game's life, holding through one quiet stretch might be the moment you become the Eternal Holder. Every long hold carries a micro-chance of winning the whole project. That's a tail-risk reward that no other NFT game offers.
  • The project has closure. It's not an abandoned Discord in 2029. It's a finite artwork with a final chapter. The whole thing becomes a completable story.
  • The "Savior" NFT creates revival drama. During Last Supper, anyone can become the person who brought the potato back from the dead. That's a once-ever role. Enormously memetic.
  • The Eternal Potato becomes a legendary artifact. A 1/1 NFT that ended a multi-year on-chain game. That's the kind of provenance collectors pay serious money for.
  • Secondary value of all souvenirs compounds. Once minting stops, the collection is finite. Legendaries become genuinely limited. Every holder benefits.

7. On-Chain Implementation Sketch

enum GameState { ACTIVE, LAST_SUPPER, SEALED }
GameState public state = GameState.ACTIVE;

uint256 public lastSupperStart;
uint256 public consecutiveTreasuryFires;

// Called at end of every buyPotato() and harvest()
function _resetFireCounter() internal { consecutiveTreasuryFires = 0; }

function treasuryBuyback() external {
    require(state == GameState.ACTIVE, "not active");
    require(block.timestamp >= lastSaleTime + 30 days, "too soon");
    // ... do the buyback ...
    consecutiveTreasuryFires++;
    if (consecutiveTreasuryFires >= 3) {
        _enterLastSupper();
    }
}

function _enterLastSupper() internal {
    state = GameState.LAST_SUPPER;
    lastSupperStart = block.timestamp;
    emit LastSupperBegan(block.timestamp, currentHolder);
}

function currentAskingPrice() public view returns (uint256) {
    if (state == GameState.LAST_SUPPER) {
        uint256 elapsed = block.timestamp - lastSupperStart;
        if (elapsed >= 30 days) return 0;
        return lastSalePrice * (30 days - elapsed) / 30 days;
    }
    // ... normal sprout/active price logic ...
}

function buyPotato(uint256 newAsk) external payable {
    require(state != GameState.SEALED, "sealed");
    if (state == GameState.LAST_SUPPER) {
        _mintSaviorNFT(msg.sender);
        state = GameState.ACTIVE;
        consecutiveTreasuryFires = 0;
    }
    // ... normal buy logic ...
}

function finalSeal() external {
    require(state == GameState.LAST_SUPPER, "not in last supper");
    require(block.timestamp >= lastSupperStart + 30 days, "too soon");
    _upgradePotatoToEternal(currentHolder);
    _mintEternalSouvenir(currentHolder);
    payable(currentHolder).transfer(pot + treasury);
    pot = 0;
    treasury = 0;
    state = GameState.SEALED;
    emit GameSealed(currentHolder);
}

8. Summary

QuestionAnswer
Does the seller get sale price + the Pot %?Yes. 95% of sale price + Pot slice (0–15% based on tier) + souvenir NFT. Three separate payouts, one transaction.

What happens during a quiet week?Nothing unusual. Pot slice grows, sit-tier climbs.

What happens if the game truly dies?The Last Supper. 30-day glowing countdown. Anyone can be the Savior and revive it. If no one does: the current holder becomes the Eternal Holder, receives Pot + Treasury + the Eternal Potato NFT, and the contract is permanently sealed.

One line to remember: the Pot says "you earn while you sit." The Last Supper says "if the music ever fully stops, whoever's sitting keeps it all." Every moment in between is a tension between those two promises.
