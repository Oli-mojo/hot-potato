🥔 HOT POTATO

v4 Mechanic Spec — Locked 2026-04-20

Direction: A + partial C · Supersedes decay-based sprouting · Single source of truth for v4 Solidity, backend, and site copy.

0. Why this exists

Earlier drafts had sprouting decay the asking price over time. On review, this created a backwards emotional arc: the longer you held, the bigger your Pot slice grew, but the more vulnerable you became — your reward and your punishment scaled together. The fix: sprouting should feel like fortification and anticipation, not erosion.

Design principle. Sprouting is the holder's story of climbing toward a terminal payout. Each stage makes them harder to dislodge, not easier. The tension is "will I survive to the Last Supper?" — not "watch my defenses melt."

1. The core loop (one paragraph)

A holder sits on the throne. Every second they hold, the throne timer ticks up and they advance through sit-tiers (Cold → Warm → Hot → Blazing → Legendary). At fixed hold-time thresholds they also advance through sprout stages (Fresh → Leafing → Sprouted → Bloomed → Harvest → Eternal), and each stage ratchets up the minimum next ask, fortifying their position. When someone does snipe them, the seller takes 95% of the sale price plus a small tier-slice of the live Pot. Meanwhile an equivalent slice accrues into a separate Last Supper Reserve that only pays out when the game seals — at which point whoever is on the throne collects a tier-weighted share and the residual rolls into Season 2.

2. Locked parameters

2.1 Sit-tier schedule (on-sale slice)

Awarded to the outgoing seller from the live Pot at the moment of sale. Halved from earlier drafts to leave room for the Reserve.

Sit tierTime on throneOn-sale Pot sliceReserve accrues
Cold< 6 h0%0%
Warm6h – 48h0.5%0.5%
Hot2 – 7 days1.5%1.5%
Blazing7 – 30 days3.5%3.5%
Legendary30+ days7.5%7.5%

Reserve accrual is funded from the 1% Pot tax on each sale and the 50% of overpay that currently routes to Pot; half of those streams are diverted into the Reserve. The holder never feels a second fee — the split happens inside the Pot accounting.

2.2 Sprout stage floor-lift changed

Each sprout stage multiplies the minimum next ask (on top of the existing tiered min-increase). Cumulative, not per-day. The effect is that a deeply sprouted potato becomes genuinely expensive to take over, which is the point.

StageUnlocks at hold timeFloor lift (cumulative)Emoji
Fresh0+0%🥔
Leafing3 days+10%🌱
Sprouted7 days+25%🌿
Bloomed14 days+50%🌾
Harvest30 days+100%🌻
Eternal60 days+200%✨

Worked example. Base price 1 ETH with the 1-10 ETH tier's +10% minimum bump = 1.1 ETH floor. At Bloomed (+50% cumulative lift): floor becomes 1.1 × 1.50 = 1.65 ETH. At Eternal: 1.1 × 3.00 = 3.30 ETH. The holder is fortified; the sniper must commit.

2.3 Last Supper trigger

The game enters LAST_SUPPER state when either condition fires, whichever comes first:

  • Timer path. 30 days without a transfer. A permanent squatter gets a natural death — the throne is eventually called in.
  • Spectator path. Staked glance-votes cross a threshold (pegged to a % of the current Reserve, e.g. 10%). Gives spectators real agency: collectively pay to force a turnover.

Once in LAST_SUPPER, a short window (48h) opens for one last round of snipes. At the end of that window, finalSeal() can be called by anyone and the Reserve pays out.

2.4 Last Supper payout (tier-weighted to the current sitter)

At seal, the sitter's final tier determines what share of the Reserve they collect. The residual rolls forward.

Final tier at sealSitter's share of ReserveResidual (rolls to Season 2)
Cold10%90%
Warm25%75%
Hot50%50%
Blazing75%25%
Legendary100%0%

A holder who got sniped at 29 days 23 hours and was replaced by a Cold sitter at seal leaves 90% of the Reserve on the table. That's the drama. "Don't let a newbie take the crown in the final hour."

3. State machine

enum GameState { ACTIVE, LAST_SUPPER, SEALED }
enum Stage      { FRESH, LEAFING, SPROUTED, BLOOMED, HARVEST, ETERNAL }

ACTIVE --(30d no transfer  OR  glance-vote threshold hit)--> LAST_SUPPER
LAST_SUPPER --(48h window ends + finalSeal() called)--> SEALED
SEALED       (terminal — Season 2 spawns fresh potato)

4. Contract-level deltas vs. existing v3

  • New storage: gameState, currentStage, stageEnteredAt, pot, reserve, lastTransferAt, lastSupperStartedAt, stageFloorLiftBps[6], stageThresholds[6], stageURIs[6], tierSaleSliceBps[5], tierReserveSliceBps[5], tierFinalShareBps[5].
  • New functions: pokeStage() (permissionless stage advance), minNextAsk() (tiered + floor-lifted), voteLastSupper() (spectator early-trigger), beginLastSupper(), finalSeal().
  • Modified buyPotato(): rejects if SEALED; calls pokeStage() at entry so every sale self-heals the stage; uses minNextAsk() for price validation; splits the 1% Pot tax and 50% of overpay equally between pot and reserve; pays the seller their on-sale tier slice from pot only; resets stage + timers; updates lastTransferAt.
  • No decay logic anywhere. effectivePrice() is removed; replaced with minNextAsk() which is monotonically non-decreasing within a hold.

5. Example game arc (narrative sanity check)

Day 0. Alice snipes the potato for 0.5 ETH, sets her ask at 1.0 ETH. Fresh stage. Pot and Reserve both sit empty.

Day 3. Alice sprouts to Leafing. Min next ask lifts +10%. She's now "Hot" tier (past 48h). If she got sniped right now she'd collect a 1.5% slice of the live Pot — but the Reserve is also building in parallel.

Day 14. Alice reaches Bloomed and Legendary. Floor is now +50% cumulative, so even minor snipe attempts bounce. She's collecting 7.5% of the Pot on sale, and 7.5% of every sale is banking into her theoretical final payout if she's still sitting at seal.

Day 27. Bob finally commits: pays 4.5 ETH (well above the sprouted floor), takes the throne. Alice walks away with 95% × 4.5 = 4.275 ETH + 7.5% of the live Pot. Bob is now Cold-tier, Fresh-stage, with only 3 days on the no-transfer clock reset.

Day 57. Bob has squatted. Nobody's bought. The 30-day no-transfer timer fires. LAST_SUPPER begins. Bob's final tier is Legendary (he's been sitting 30 days). He holds on. 48h window passes. finalSeal() — Bob collects 100% of the Reserve. Season 1 is over.

Alternate Day 57. A late snipe knocks Bob off at hour 47. A newcomer "Cold" at seal only collects 10% of the Reserve. 90% rolls to Season 2's starting Reserve — Season 2 opens with a fat pot already on the table.

  • Pot widget gains a sibling: Last Supper Reserve, shown alongside the live Pot with its own dollar estimate.
  • Tier ladder shows two numbers per row: on-sale slice and reserve accrual. Adds a column for "final share at seal."
  • FAQ answer on squatting changes from "sprouting decays their price" to "sprouting fortifies them, but the 30-day no-transfer timer forces an endgame anyway, and spectators can vote to force it earlier."
