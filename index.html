THE RULES
🔥
Always For Sale
The Hot Potato is always listed. The current owner cannot delist or refuse a sale. Anyone can buy at any time.
📈
Price Only Goes Up
Every buyer must set a new asking price at least 15% above what they paid. Enforced on-chain — no exceptions. Overpay above the minimum and you'll get a rarity boost on your souvenir when you're eventually bought out.
🎁
You Can't Sell
You don't choose when to exit. When someone buys from you, the contract automatically sends your ETH and mints your souvenir. You just wait.
👑
Hold Longer. Get Rarer.
Your souvenir rarity depends on how long you held. Under 24h = Common. 3+ months = 50% chance of Legendary.

Your souvenir rarity starts from how long you hold — but four things can push it higher. Overpaying above the 15% minimum bumps your tier at purchase time. Promo codes (from Discord, X, or community events) stack on top. Trade in a souvenir you already own to convert it into a single-use boost code. And loyal holders — wallets that have held the potato before — automatically get a bonus when they get bought out again. All boosts stack, capped at Legendary.

Hold Time (Base)
Your starting rarity comes from how long you held. Under 24h = mostly Common. Hold 3+ months and you're in Legendary territory. No action needed — just wait.
💸
Overpay Boost
Set your new ask price higher than the 15% minimum when you buy. Pay 10–24% over min → +1 tier. 25–49% over → +2. 50–99% → +3. 100%+ → +4 (max).
🎟️
Promo Codes
Enter a code in the buy modal before purchasing. Social codes (DISCORD, XFOLLOW) give +1. Trade-in codes scale up to +4. Use 🔥 Heat Up Your Rarity in the buy modal.
🔥
Trade-In Codes
Own a souvenir? Burn it from the gallery to get a single-use code. Common → +1, Rare → +2, Epic → +3, Legendary → +4. A sacrifice for a future legend.
🏆
Loyalty Boost
Held the potato before? You earn an automatic boost next time you're bought out — no code needed. 1 prior hold → +1. 2 holds → +2. 3+ holds → +3 (max loyalty).
🤝
Referral Boost
Share your referral code from the buy modal. When someone uses it, both of you get +1 added to your next souvenir. Find your code after connecting your wallet.
