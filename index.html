THE RULES
🔥
Always For Sale
The Hot Potato is always listed. The current owner cannot delist or refuse a sale. Anyone can buy at any time.
📈
Price Only Goes Up
Every buyer must set a new asking price above what they paid. Under 0.1 ETH the minimum is +25%. It scales down to +15%, +10%, +7%, +5% as price rises. Enforced on-chain — no exceptions. Overpay above the minimum and you bank a rarity boost on your future souvenir.
🎁
You Have No Choice
You don't decide when to exit. When someone buys from you, the contract automatically sends your ETH and mints your souvenir. You just wait.
👑
Legends Are Earned.
Hold for 7+ days and you're in Legendary territory. Overpay on entry to boost even further. No shortcuts — the contract enforces it all.

Your souvenir rarity is determined by how long you held — but four things can push it higher. Overpaying above the minimum when you buy stores a boost that applies to your souvenir when you eventually sell. Promo codes (from Discord, X, or community events) stack on top. Trade in a souvenir you already own to convert it into a single-use boost code. And loyal holders — wallets that have held the potato before — automatically get a bonus when they get bought out again. All boosts stack, capped at Legendary.

⏱️
Hold Time (Base)
Your starting rarity comes from how long you held. Under 6h = mostly Common. Hold 7+ days and you're in Legendary territory. No action needed — just wait.
💸
Overpay Boost
Pay more than the asking price when you buy — the contract stores a boost for your future souvenir. 10–24% over → +1 tier. 25–49% → +2. 50–99% → +3. 100%+ → +4 (max).
🎟️
Promo Codes
Enter a code in the buy modal before purchasing. Social codes (DISCORD, XFOLLOW) give +1. Trade-in codes scale up to +4. Use 🔥 Heat Up Your Rarity in the buy modal.
🔥
Trade-In Codes
Own a souvenir? Burn it from the gallery to get a single-use code. Common → +1, Rare → +2, Epic → +3, Legendary → +4. A sacrifice for a future legend.
🏆
Loyalty Boost
Held the potato before? You earn an automatic boost next time you're bought out — no code needed. 1 prior hold → +1. 2 holds → +2. 3+ holds → +3 (max loyalty).
🤝
Referral Boost
Share your referral code from the buy modal. When someone uses it, both of you get +1 added to your next souvenir. Find your code after connecting your wallet.

Decentralized & Autonomous

Hot Potato is a fully on-chain game deployed on the Base blockchain. The smart contract is the sole arbiter of all game rules. No individual, company, or entity controls or can alter the outcome of any transaction once submitted.

Transactions Are Irreversible

All purchases are final. Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed, cancelled, or refunded. Once you send a transaction and it is confirmed on-chain, your ETH is transferred and the contract executes automatically. There are no exceptions.
